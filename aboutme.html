About Me

Girish Dodda alias mrblacklicorice

Simple beginnings

My initial contact with programming and computer science in general started in the winter of 2018. It was winter break and my uncle(Who currently works at microsoft) came over for the weekend. On the night of his second day, he explained the basics of computer science and memory in 3 hours. I was absolutely amazed at how a complex creation like a computer is based on simple principles at its foundation. He also introduced me into C on the very night and intrigued me. I didn't know it then but that was one of the best presents I got for the holiday.
